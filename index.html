Bikes are more than just a mode of transport — they represent freedom, adventure, and a greener way to move. Whether you're cruising through city streets or exploring rugged trails, bikes offer a perfect blend of performance, style, and sustainability for every rider.

Honda CBR1000RR-R Fireblade Sports Bike The Honda CBR1000RR-R Fireblade is a high-performance supersport motorcycle engineered for the track but built for the road. Powered by a 999cc inline-four engine, it delivers an astonishing 214.6 hp (160 kW) at 14,500 rpm and 113 Nm of torque at 12,500 rpm. With MotoGP-inspired aerodynamics, advanced electronics, and razor-sharp handling, the Fireblade is built for riders who demand elite speed, precision, and racing DNA.
Kawasaki Ninja H2 Sports Bike The Kawasaki Ninja H2 is a hyper-sport motorcycle that combines cutting-edge technology with breathtaking performance. Powered by a supercharged 998cc inline-four engine, it produces an astonishing 228 hp (170 kW) at 11,500 rpm and 137 Nm of torque at 11,000 rpm. With its aerodynamic design, advanced electronics, and track-ready capabilities, the Ninja H2 is engineered for riders who crave extreme speed and precision on the road or track.
Ducati Panigale V4 Sports Bike Ducati Panigale V4 is a high-performance superbike that combines race-bred technology with Italian design. At its heart is a 1103cc Desmosedici Stradale V4 engine, producing an astonishing 214 horsepower at 13,000 rpm. With its advanced electronics, aerodynamic styling, and MotoGP-inspired engineering, the Panigale V4 delivers unmatched precision, speed, and exhilaration on both road and track.
Harley Davidson 48 Cruiser Harley-Davidson Fat Boy is a legendary cruiser that blends classic American styling with modern muscle. At its core lies a powerful Milwaukee-Eight 114 V-Twin engine, delivering a strong 94 horsepower and 119 lb-ft of torque. With its iconic design, solid-disc wheels, and signature rumble, the Fat Boy offers a ride defined by presence, torque, and freedom on the open road.
HONDA Gold Wing Cruiser Honda Gold Wing is a premium touring cruiser that epitomizes long-distance luxury and cutting-edge technology. At its heart lies a smooth and powerful 1833cc six-cylinder engine, producing a refined 125 horsepower and 170 Nm of torque. Equipped with advanced features like Apple CarPlay, electronically adjustable suspension, and reverse gear with walking mode, the Gold Wing offers unmatched comfort, stability, and touring capability for riders who demand the best on the open road.
BMW R18 Cruiser BMW R 18 is a bold cruiser that blends heritage-inspired styling with modern engineering. At its core is a massive 1802cc air/oil-cooled boxer engine, delivering a commanding 91 horsepower and 158 Nm of torque at low revs. With its classic double-loop steel frame, shaft drive, and retro cruiser aesthetics, the R 18 offers a ride defined by raw torque, timeless design, and German precision — paying homage to BMW’s legendary 1936 R 5.
